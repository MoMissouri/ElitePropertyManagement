OUR GOal

We are here for the long term, our mission is to create relationship that leaves a lasting impression. We know we're new in the market however with us you will get exceptional tailored service that is suited for your property and yourself.

Elite Property Management aims to make your life more simple while ensuring you are not losing money by being over charged for the day to day running of your property. We do this by charging a fixed yearly rate, only charging you what we are charged by our contractors and no fixed term length of contract.

OUR Process


Step 1 - (Negotiate)
A survey will be conducted at the property. Once completed we will negotiate a price which is suitable for both parties.

Step 2 - (Acquire)    
Contracts will be made between the home owner and elite property. Once signed we Will collect the keys and do the necessary work needed to get the property ready to rent.
‍
Step 3 - (Manage)
Once the property is ready we will find rightful tenants to fill the property.
‍
Step 4 - (Relax and let the money come in)
We will take care of the rest. Once the property is filled with tenants just relax and receive your payment guaranteed every month. We will handle the day to day, any problem we will deal with, any work or maintenance needed will be handled for free with the £100 maintenance so you have as little to deal with as possible.
